I Wish I Was a Bigfoot

I Wish I Was a Bigfoot is the story of a young girl celebrating her seventh birthday who has grown tired of the trials and tribulations of growing up. Convinced that Bigfoots live an easy, carefree life, she dreams of leaving her ordinary world behind to join the mysterious creatures of the forest. On her birthday, her wish to escape life’s challenges reaches its peak as she blows out her candles, believing she will be transformed into a Bigfoot—along with all her worries. But as she soon discovers, even the most magical wishes can come with unexpected surprises. Be careful what you wish for!

Ideal for ages 3–7 Perfect for birthday read-alouds

Caterpillar’s Garden

Caterpillar’s Garden is the story of a tiny creature who stumbles upon a garden gone to ruin. With the help of the animals who live there, the caterpillar works to restore new life to the grounds—despite the dangers hidden among the grasses and trees. Filled with themes of bravery, confidence, and transformation, the story reflects the hope and renewal found in Christ’s resurrection and ascension.

Ideal for ages 3–7 Faith-inspired themes
